Powerful Commands

Integrated seamlessly with Nightbot or any webfetch-capable bot.

User Stats

Detailed ranking and history for any user in your chat.

!stats

"User is ranked #42 with 1,234 messages. First seen 150 days ago."

Top Rankings

Showcase the most active members of your community.

!top 5

"1. UserA: 5000 | 2. UserB: 4500..."

Stream Streaks

Track consecutive stream attendance for users.

!streak

"@User is on a 15 stream streak! Present in 45/50 total streams."

Word Search

Find when a word was first said or how many times.

!wordcount "pog"

"'pog' has been said 15,234 times in chat."

The First Time

Find the exact moment and person who first said a word.

!firstsaid "hello"

"'hello' was first said by UserX 200 days ago. [Link]"

The OG Cult

List the absolute first members who joined your chat.

!oldest 5

"1. UserA: 500 days ago | 2. UserB: 498 days ago..."

How to Install

Setting up is as simple as copying these commands into your chat.

Copy and paste these commands into your YouTube or Twitch chat where Nightbot is an editor:

!addcom !streak $(urlfetch https://stats.streamsnip.com/streak) !addcom !top $(urlfetch https://stats.streamsnip.com/top/$(query)) !addcom !stats $(urlfetch https://stats.streamsnip.com/stats) !addcom !wordcount $(urlfetch https://stats.streamsnip.com/wordcount/$(querystring)) !addcom !oldest $(urlfetch https://stats.streamsnip.com/oldest/$(query)) !addcom !youngest $(urlfetch https://stats.streamsnip.com/youngest/$(query)) !addcom !firstsaid $(urlfetch https://stats.streamsnip.com/firstsaid/$(querystring))
